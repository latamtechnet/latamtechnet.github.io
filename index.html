Connecting Tech and Latin America

LatamTech is a Network for sharing ideas, contacts and knowledge related to the Latin American tech ecosystem. We are a group of Harvard and MIT students and alumni who support technological adoption in Latin-American industries and governments.

Get involved

What is our goal?

Our number one goal is to boost the growth of the Latin-American tech landscape through creating a space that allows for a rich interaction between researchers, professionals and policy-makers connected to the field.


Get involved!

If you are in the region and want to be part of the network or receive mentorship and tech support from its members, contact us. If you are a student, a researcher or an entrepreneur in Cambridge, come and join our talks! Finally, if you have an interesting project that you would like to develop, check out our new Latam Data Fellows initiative!

LatamTech Strengths

Connections

We connect people in the network to the key players in technology and policy making. If you are an entrepreneur, an investor or a policy maker working with tech in the region, we want to get to know you. Our meetings connect innovation opportunities across the region.

Knowledge

We share the latest news, research papers and interesting ideas in the tech world. Our current focus is the Artificial Intelligence agenda for Latin America.

Mentorship

Latam Tech members are all about supporting innovations in the region. If you are trying to launch a tech-driven project and need support, this is the place to find an amazing pool of talent.
