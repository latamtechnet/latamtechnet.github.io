Connecting Latin America

LatamTech is a Network for sharing ideas, contacts and knowledge related to the Latin American tech atmosphere. We connect the key players in technology, policy making and industry to provide a rich platform where ideas flow and projects become reality.

What is LatamTech?

LatamTech is a network of researchers, professionals, students and policy makers interested in pushing the boundaries of the Latin American technology ecosystem.


What is our goal?

Our number one goal is to boost the growth of the Latin American tech landscape. We plan to do that through a medium that allows for a rich interaction between researchers, professionals and policy-makers connected to the field.


How can we help?

We would love to hear about your concerns, ideas or needs related to the latin american tech scene. Please leave a comment on the feedback box.
